Hamburger Of The Week

Time

Level: Easy
Prep: 10 min
Yeild: 4 Hamburgers
Total: 25 min
Cook: 15 min

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces ground chuck
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Spice Mix, optional, recipe follows
  • 1 tablespoon vegitable oil
  • 4 slices cheese, such as Cheddar, American Cheese
  • 4 soft hamburger-style buns, split
  • 4 slices beefsteak tomatoes, optional
  • Assorted lettuces and greens, such as iceberg
  • Assorted mustards, optional
  • Mayonnaise, optional
  • Ketchup, optional

    • Spice Mix

  • 1 tablespoon Worcesterchire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • Hot Sauce, to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F. PLace a roasting rack on a foil-lines baking sheet in the oven
  2. Using your hands, break the meat into small piees and combine evenly but loosely on a parchment or waxed paper-lines baking sheet. Spread the meat out and season it generously with salt and pepper. If desired, add the spice mix at this time.
  3. Divide the meat into 4 portions (about 6 ounces each). Using your hands, form each portion into a ball-shape by gently tossing it from 1 hand to the other. (Don't over work or press to firmly on the meat.) Gently form each portion into a patty about 3 1/2 inches wide and 1-inch-thick.
  4. Preheat a large cast iron skillet over medium-low heat for 5 minutes. Raise the heat to high and add the oil. Add the patties and cook, turning once, until well-browned, about 2 minutes each side.
  5. Using a spatula, transfer the hambergers to the roasting rack in the oven and continue cooking to desired doneness, 8 to 9 minutes for medium-rare, 10 to 11 minutes for medium, and 13 ti 15 minutes for well-done. If you are using the cheese, top the hambergers during their last couple of minutes of cooking to melt.
  6. Transfer the hambergers to a plate, let rest for a couple minutes before serving. Meanwhile, toast the hamberger buns. Assemble the hamburgers whith the condiments and toppings of your choice. serve
  7. When cooking on a countertop grill, turn on grill, then place burgers on the heated grill.

    8. Timing for countertop grill:

  8. For Medium-Rare: Cook for 3 minutes covered and then unplug the machine and continue to cook for 2 to 3 more minutes.
  9. For Medium: Cook for 4 minutes covered and then unplug the machine and continue to cook covered for 2 to 3 more minutes
  10. For Well: Cook for 5 minutes covered and then unplug the machine
